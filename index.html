One Family One Clinic

All the care your family needs — all under one roof. From your infant’s first dental exam to teeth whitening before your wedding day, you’ll be glad Bite an Apple is where you chose to call home for your whole family’s dental health needs.

Your treatment is tailored to you, nothing is cookie-cutter or one-size-fits-all. We will work closely with you to create the treatment plan that addresses your concerns and prepares you for a lifetime of smiles.

Dr. Bhavna Suri

Born in Delhi, Dr. Bhavna Suri did her Bachelors of Dental Surgery from DAV Yamunagar, Harayana.For several years, she taught graduating students in general dentistry: restorative (fillings), fixed and removable prosthodontics (crown and bridge), orthodontics (braces and Invisalign), pediatrics, oral surgery, periodontics (soft tissue/gums), endodontics (root canals), cosmetic and implant therapy.

During this time, she realized that dentistry needed a different approach. This was her calling to start “Bite an apple” – a modern dentistry with a conservation approach. Do not sell any treatment, which the patient does not need, is the core value to this centre.

With a soft touch, engaging personality and attention to detail, Dr. Bhavna prides herself in establishing a natural rapport with her patients. She truly believes in patient-centered care.

Our Services

Dental Cleaning & Exam

Using state-of-the-art tools and technology, Our dentistry professionals remove built-up plaque and tartar then polishes your enamel for a bright, healthy smile.

Scaling and Root Planing

Scaling and root planing is a deep periodontal cleaning that can be performed in our clinic with local anesthesia to numb the areas being treated.

Teeth Whitening

Let us brighten your smile! Our whitenings are both easy and non-invasive, and you can remove years of stain and color from your teeth in a single treatment. The question for you is, how white do you want your teeth to be? Natural white? Extra bright? Brilliant? Our dentists will show you your options.

Bonding & White Filling

Cavities can happen to anyone, even those with perfect dental hygiene habits. But while a small cavity is nothing to get too worked up about, it should be addressed ASAP.

Dental Sealants

Dental sealants are a thin, protective coating (made from plastic or other dental materials) that easily adheres to the chewing surface of your back teeth.

Dental Emergencies

Even the best cared for teeth occasionally need a little help. Loose fillings, broken crowns, falling bridges, and more… no matter the need or emergency, Bite An Apple has you fully covered

Root Canal Treatments

Root canals are probably the most notorious procedure in dentistry, but also the most misunderstood. While many people view them as “scary,” the truth is that a root canal is an effective treatment to stop pain and save your tooth.

Tooth Extraction

Our first priority is to save your tooth, but when tooth damage is so extensive that it cannot be repaired, a dental extraction may be necessary.

Implants

When you’re dealing with missing, broken, or severely damaged teeth, your confidence can suffer. More importantly, these issues take a toll on your health. You deserve better. Talk to us about dental implant restorations.

Bridges

Have you lost one or more teeth? You may be a great candidate for a dental bridge. A well-crafted porcelain bridge looks just like your natural teeth and restores your smile to its original health and function.

Crowns

A dental crown is like a cap that covers the entire visible portion of the tooth, protecting its remaining structure and bringing back its original shape, size, and color.

Dentures

If you’re missing multiple teeth due to decay, injury, or the natural aging process, dentures might be the perfect solution.

Invisalign

Gone are the days of wires, rubber bands and food stuck in brackets. We have partnered with Invisalign to provide a series of clear retainers that deliver proven results in less than half the time, at half the average cost, of traditional braces.

Veneers

If you have cosmetic concerns about your teeth, porcelain veneers are one of the quickest, most dramatic ways to improve your smile.

Gum Disease

Most Gum Diseases can be treated via non-surgical methods. Vist us to know your options.

TMD and TMJ Treatments

Do you suffer from migraines or migraine-type headaches? Does your jaw feel tight or produce a clicking noise every time you open your mouth? Suffer from neck pain or frequent earaches? Tell your dentist!

Full Mouth Rehab

A full mouth rehab treatment requires careful planning and combines multiple treatments for optimal results. We’ll address problems, protect your teeth, and restore the beauty of your smile and ensure your comfort from start to finish.

Family Dentistry

Bite an apple is a family dentistry which provides general, surgical, and cosmetic care for people of all ages. Our team specializes in helping younger kids, teens, and adults develop a great relationship with their dentist.
