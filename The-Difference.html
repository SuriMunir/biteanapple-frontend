Why Us - The Difference

Bite An Apple

Know why we are your best dental treatement option

The Difference is You

We’re always looking for the best way to do things. More importantly, we look for the best solution for you. Your treatment is tailored to you, nothing is cookie-cutter or one-size-fits-all. We will work closely with you to create the treatment plan that addresses your concerns and prepares you for a lifetime of smiles.

Every mouth welcome, No Judgement

Your health is important — no matter your age, income, or dental health history. Enjoy an inclusive approach and high-quality care backed by years of experience and expertise. Tired of feeling stressed out, anxious, or annoyed at the thought of going to the dentist? If managing your family’s dental care (let alone your own) feels like pulling teeth, it’s time for a change.

Transparancy matters

Get only the care that you need, and the options you select—nothing more, nothing less. Our ethos is modern dentistry but conservative approach. We will let you know what you really need and what is cosmetic in nature. You decide what you want. Our pricing is also transparent -- no hidden charges!!

Right Technology matters

Dentistry is both art and science. We employ the latest technologies in dentistry, continually train ourselves on the latest methodologies and best-practices, and pride ourselves on partnering with the best clinical teams. We are also aware that technology is not a gimick but the right investments give right results.

Experience matters

Dr. Bhavna has more than 2 decades of experience, thousands of happy patients and greatful families. She has further created and is supported by an eco system of the highest rated clinical teams in Gurgaon! Apart from the experience it is the genuine care with which Dr. Bhavna treats her patients.

Safety Comes First

You’re in good hands. Find peace of mind with a built-in air purification system, medical-grade sterilization protocols, clean patient rooms, and the latest and greatest dental tech. We follow strict covid safety protocols. All our staff is checked daily to keep you safe.

Care on schedule

Book a visit, complete paperwork…all online. Text, email, or call us. Be confident that your visit will run on time — get in and out efficiently and safely. We have real respect for your time.

Affordable Dental Care

Our core philosphy is that one does not have to be rich to afford excellent state of the art dental care. We have found a price point that allows our working class patients the ability to have access to care they would otherwise become unaffordable. “The more patients that can afford treatment, means the more we get to do what we love.”

Community Matters

Once every quarter we conduct free children dental camp. The camp is for 2 days in which all children from different schools come to the clinic for a free dental treatment. Our endeavour is to have every child in palam vihar to have the healthiest mouth possible. To support this we give 5% of our income towards this initiative. We also collect from our network of friends and family to make this a success.
